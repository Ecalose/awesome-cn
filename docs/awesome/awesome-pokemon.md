## Development Projects
#### APIs
- [PokeAPI](https://github.com/PokeAPI/pokeapi/) -神奇宝贝的RESTful API（http://pokeapi.co/）.

- [PokemonGO-Pokedex](https://github.com/Biuni/PokemonGO-Pokedex) -JSON中的PokémonGO的Pokédex.
